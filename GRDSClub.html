Ina Saltz, “Body Type”

Former Time magazine design director Ina Saltz is a Professor Emeritus of Digital Design at the City College of New York, and the author of eight LinkedIn Learning courses focussing on typography and letterforms. She is the author of the Body Type series of books on typographic tattoos, as well as Typography Essentials: 100 Design Principles for Working with Type. Her online courses have had almost three million views.
